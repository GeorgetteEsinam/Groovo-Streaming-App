# 🎶 Groovo – Music Streaming App

Welcome to **Groovo**, a beautifully designed dark mode–only music streaming app built for those who live and breathe music. Whether you're vibing to smooth jazz, getting hyped with hip-hop, or chilling with lo-fi beats, Groovo is your daily music companion.

## 🌌 Why Groovo?

Music isn’t just something we listen to—it’s something we feel. Groovo was designed with that emotion in mind. From the slick dark-themed interface to the immersive audio experience, every detail is meant to keep you in the zone, day or night.

## ✨ Features at a Glance

- 🎵 **Unlimited Streaming** – Play any song, anytime
- 📁 **Personalized Playlists** – Create your own vibes
- 🔍 **Smart Search** – Find songs, albums, and artists fast
- 📊 **Trending & New Releases** – Stay in the loop with what's hot
- ❤️ **Favorites** – Like your favorite tracks for quick access
- 🎚️ **High-Quality Audio** – Crystal-clear playback experience
- 🌓 **Always Dark Mode** – Designed to be easy on your eyes
- 📱 **Mobile-First Design** – Fully responsive and smooth on any device
- 🔊 **Background Playback** – Listen while using other apps
